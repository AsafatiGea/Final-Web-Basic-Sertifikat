Welcome to Nias Island

Nias Island is one of the best tourist destinations in Indonesia. It is famous for its world-class waves, unique cultural heritage, and stunning natural landscapes that remain beautifully preserved.

Why Visit Nias?

World-Class Surfing

Sorake Beach offers some of the best waves in the world.

Unique Culture

Experience traditional stone jumping and iconic Nias houses.

Beautiful Nature

Enjoy pristine beaches, traditional villages, and stunning scenery.
